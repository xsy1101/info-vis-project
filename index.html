Unnumbered

Am I just a number
a dot on a graph
Confined to a cell made of lines

Or something with a heartbeat
Flesh and bone trembling with a pulse
A homage to those who came before me
Tied to this world by other hearts that beats in tandem

When you came you brought pain and misery
An empire built on the back of slaves
Endless rivers of blood you pollute this land
that you call yours

Take me
away from the endless sky
and those who love me
We are not voiceless
And we will scream till the end

One day
History will know
and I will be home
